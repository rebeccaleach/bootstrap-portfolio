About Me

I love stories, writing code, and learning about new tech. As a kid, I entertained myself by making up stories in my head and taking apart my electronics to see how they worked. Eventually, I started scribbling my stories down in notebooks and hacking up the code in my first blog. Now I edit novels and magazines, design for print and the web, and study programming languages.

Over the course of my career, I’ve worked in graphic design, book publishing, software documentation, and financial services as an editor, technical writer, designer, and presentation specialist. As an undergraduate, I studied writing and information design. I earned my master’s degree in writing from the New School University in 2011 and was a 2013 Lambda Literary Fellow, studying with Malinda Lo.

When not working or learning new coding languages, you can usually find me writing young adult novels about queer girls from the future or geeking out about outer space (especially Mars). I also love training on the static and flying trapezes. Currently, I live in Austin, Texas with my four cats, one dog, and a hamster who is surprisingly chill considering his carnivorous roommates.
